Blox Fruits is a Roblox game inspired by One Piece, offering the opportunity to explore different seas and islands. Youcan either become a blox fruit "devil fruit" user to acquire unique powers or opt for swords to become a skilled swordsman.

The Strongest Battlegrounds is a Roblox fighting game that draws inspiration from iconic anime and manga characters for its combat. Players can train their own original character or become one from the One-Punch Man world, engaging in immersive PvP battles.
